# Namassa App

Namassa is a prototype iOS application developed by students from the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Cear√° during the Foundation class of the Apple Developer Academy. The app is based on the premise of offering a practical way of finding recipes based on the user's availability of ingredients. To this end, a filtering search engine is provided, allowing recipes to be found based on the user's choices.

# Main features :joystick:

## Ingredient selection flow

With this feature, the user can define, by category, the ingredients that should make up the recipes they are searching for. In view of the main objective of the application, it is usual for the user to select ingredients that are in some way accessible in their preparation environment.
